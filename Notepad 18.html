Conclusion & Thank You

A total of 173 plant species were collected from 20 houses in Kampung Changkat Phase 4. The plants that are most often planted and almost in every house are Cocos nucifera, Mangifera indica, and Nephelium lappaceum. Jalan Tanjung has a significantly higher number of plants and greater species diversity compared to Jalan Melati. This shows that the residents of Jalan Tanjung are more involved in the cultivation and maintenance of various plants in their homes and yards.
